About Us

VIDA MASTERCOAT (VMC)

We manufacture and supply advanced nanotechnology thermal insulation paint, industrial coatings, and construction chemical materials for residential, commercial, and industrial purposes under our registered trademark VIDA MASTERCOAT.

Has been steadily growing due to its ability to provide the right products that fulfil the everincreasing demands of the Construction Industry, Industrial, and Housing Projects. VMC is committed to creating specific solutions, forgoing key business partnerships, and acquiring a diverse range of exceptional clients.

Using advanced R&D infrastructure and quality materials, we have developed several products that meet and compete with international standards requirements.

One of its goals is to provide economical solutions and superior customer service to its clients.
