Who We Are?

Vida Industrial Company was established in Dammam by a distinguished group of engineers, administrators, and technicians with extensive scientific and technical expertise who have left their mark on the local and international industrial markets. Vida boasts a wide product line accompanied by a variety of industrial departments, each offering exceptional performance, quality, and excellence. Among them are the wrought iron and metal formation department, the sheet and pipe laser cutting department, the metal coating and electroplating department, the metal casting department, and the resin department. The foundational principles upon which our company was established include committing to innovation in performance and quality, fulfilling our customers’ vision and requirements in a unique and non-traditional manner, and leveraging our expertise to offer solutions and ideas that transform the clients’ desires into tangible realities. Furthermore, Vida places significant importance on providing after-sales services to uphold the integrity of its products and ensure the utmost customer satisfaction, peace of mind, and confidence in their future interactions with us
